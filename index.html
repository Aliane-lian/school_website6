About Fawe Girls Gahini

Welcome to FAWE Girls School Gahini, a center of excellence nestled in the serene Eastern Province of Rwanda. More than just an academic institution, we are a close-knit community dedicated to empowering young women through education, leadership, and values-driven learning.

For many years, FAWE Gahini has stood at the forefront of girls' education in Rwanda. Rooted in a commitment to academic excellence and gender equity, we combine strong classroom instruction with holistic development. Our curriculum embraces both national and international perspectives, preparing students to thrive in a dynamic world. In addition to Kinyarwanda and English, we incorporate technology and life skills to ensure our students are well-prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

At FAWE Girls School Gahini, we believe that every girl has the power to lead and inspire. Through community engagement, mentorship, and a strong moral foundation, we nurture confident, capable young women ready to make a positive impact in Rwanda and beyond.
